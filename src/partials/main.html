About the Game

A space for mental reset

Inner Weave is not about winning — it’s about slowing down. Players interact with evolving visual patterns that respond to touch, rhythm,
and breathing pace. It actually learns how you like to interact and adjusts accordingly.

How to Play

Touch, breathe, follow

  • Start with a Breath

Begin by syncing with a slow visual rhythm.

  • Interact Gently

Swipe or tap to influence the patterns.

  • Follow the Flow

Let visuals guide your pace.

  • Release Control

Just let it do its thing - don't try to control everything.

  • Stay Present

There is no goal — only experience.
